Hi, I'm Danae, a composer, tech audio designer and indie game dev from The Netherlands! I'm making games as Audune.

In my games I try to combine intriguing stories, unusual fantasy worlds, and whimsical music into an immersive game experience. In the past I've collaborated with some amazing folks to help taking my games to the next level!

My first big project has been Invisible Wings: Chapter One, a visual novel set in a modern fantasy world where faeries and humans were supposed to live together. I am currently working on Wesley Smith's Repair Game, a local coop arcade game where you collect items, craft weapons and defeat your opponents in a multitude of pixel art arenas. I've also participated in several game jams such as Global Game Jam and Ludum Dare, both as programmer and audio designer.
