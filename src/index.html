Audune Games is a small indie game developer based in The Netherlands with the mission to make immersive games with a bit of fantasy sprinkled on top.

Founded and led by Danae Dekker, Audune Games tries to combine intruiging and cute stories, unusual fantasy worlds, and the most whimsical music into an immersive game experience. But Danae isn't doing this alone! She has collaborated with some amazing folks to help taking her games to the next level!

Audune's first big project has been Invisible Wings: Chapter One, a visual novel set in a modern fantasy world where faeries and humans were supposed to live together. The team is currently working on Wesley Smith's Repair Game, a local coop arcade game where you collect items, craft weapons and defeat your opponents in a multitude of pixel art arenas.
